Events & Competitions

From intimate weekly quiz nights to high-stakes national competitions, every event is a chance to learn, compete, and celebrate knowledge.

Weekly

Quiz Nights

Our flagship weekly gathering. Every Friday, members battle it out across themes ranging from science and history to pop culture and sports. Open to all IITGN students.

Workshop

Quizcraft Sessions

Hands-on workshops on quiz setting, research methodology, and question framing. Learn from experienced quizmasters and develop your own quizzing style.

National

National Quiz Circuit

We actively participate in India's national quizzing circuit including Tata Crucible, NIHILANTH, and various inter-IIT quiz competitions, consistently representing IITGN on the national stage.

Special

Themed Quiz Marathons

Dedicated deep-dive quizzes on specific themes: Science-a-thon, Hist-o-mania, Cine-Quiz, Sports Special, and more. Perfect for enthusiasts of particular domains.

Collaboration

Fest Collaborations

Quiz events organized as part of Amalthea (tech fest) and Blithchron (cultural fest), bringing the thrill of competitive quizzing to IITGN's biggest annual celebrations.

The Quizzing Society of IIT Gandhinagar. Where curiosity meets competition, and every question is a doorway to a new world.
