Where Curiosity Meets Competition

We are the Quizzing Society of IIT Gandhinagar — a community of relentlessly curious minds who believe that every question is a doorway to a new world.

More Than Just Questions

Born from a shared love of trivia and intellectual sparring, the Quizzing Society has grown into one of the most vibrant communities at IITGN. We don't just ask questions — we cultivate a culture of curiosity that spans every discipline, from astrophysics to Bollywood, from ancient history to cutting-edge technology.

Our weekly quiz nights, themed events, and competitive circuit participation have forged a space where knowledge is celebrated, debate is encouraged, and every "I didn't know that!" moment is a small victory.

Whether you're a seasoned quizzer who has competed on the national stage or someone who just enjoys a good trivia round with friends — there's a seat for you at our table.
