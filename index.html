Where Curiosity Meets Competition

We are the Quizzing Society of IIT Gandhinagar — a community of relentlessly curious minds who believe that every question is a doorway to a new world.

More Than Just Questions

Born from a shared love of trivia and intellectual sparring, the Quizzing Society has grown into one of the most vibrant communities at IITGN. We cultivate a culture of curiosity that spans every discipline, from astrophysics to Bollywood, from ancient history to cutting-edge technology.

What We Do

Three pillars that define the Quizzing Society experience.

Weekly Trivia

Every week, we gather to test our wits across diverse topics. From casual pub-style quizzes to intense buzzer rounds, there's always something new to learn.

Major Tournaments

We host and participate in national-level inter-college tournaments. Our teams regularly travel the country to compete against the best minds in the circuit.

Knowledge Archiving

We believe in preserving knowledge. Our extensive archive of past quizzes and questions serves as a rigorous training ground for upcoming quizzers.
